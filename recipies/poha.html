Poha

Description

It is a dish consisting of flattened puffed rice grains and is the staple breakfast of the marathi people and is indeed very tasty

Whole groundnuts are also added while cooking to add flavour to the dish otherwise the taste gets rather monotonous and to top it all of we use coconut sprinkles

Ingredients

  • Flattened puffed rice
  • Groundnuts
  • Coconut sprinkles

Steps

  1. Make poha by flattend rice
  2. Add groundnut in the middle stages of preparing
  3. Sprinkle coconut sprinkles after plating
  4. You may also use out left-over farsan from misal preparation
  5. Eat!!!
