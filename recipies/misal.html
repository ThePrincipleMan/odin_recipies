Misal

Description

It consists of sprouts and pav with added garnishes on the top of sprouts in the way of yogart ,farsan and the types

You are supposed to take a piece of break and dunk it in the sprouts and encapsulate as much sprouts as possible and enjoy it that way to the fullest

Ingredients

  • Sprouts
  • Yogart
  • Farsan
  • Pav (bread)

Steps

  1. Make sprout curry
  2. Add yogart and farsan as garnish
  3. Plate the pav along with sprout
  4. Eat!!!
